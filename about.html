About us

Bounce house is a local Minnesota organization dedicated to providing inflatable moonbounces and slides to you and your family. Our goal is to help your party go as smoothly as possible. Whether it's your child's birthday, or another special time, we would like to help make it one of the most memorable experiences.

As parents, we understand that your time is valuable, and you can't always take as much time as you'd like to set up parties. That's why we will deliver and set up our products for you. This ensures that you'll be able to use all your available time with your loved ones.

We realize that your child's safety is something that you are not willing to compromise, and we make sure that all of our products are ready to handle as many kids as they are rated for.

Even before you decide to rent one of our products, you will be treated with as much respect as we show our family, as we'll consider you to be a close friend. We will do whatever it takes to be sure that your child's next party is a huge success.

If you have any questions, concerns, or comments, feel free to contact us. We'll be sure to work with you to make sure you get everything you need.
